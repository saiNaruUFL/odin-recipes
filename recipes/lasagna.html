Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is type of pasta that takes a rectangular shape, usally filled with tomato saucuing, cheese layering, and different types of meat

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Water
  • White Sugar

Steps

  1. Cook sausage,beef,and garlic onion
  2. Boil salt water. Cook lasagna noodles
  3. Set temperature to 375 degrees
  4. Apply tomato sauce, and sprinkle cheese
  5. Serve the food
